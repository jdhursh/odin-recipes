Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

You will need:

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions:

  1. Lay slices of bread on plate
  2. Spread peanut butter on left slice
  3. Spread jelly on right slice
  4. Put left side, peanut butter down, on top of the right side
  5. Flip sandwich upside down
